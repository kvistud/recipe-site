Almond Butter - Chia - Overnight oats

This one you'll have to make the night before, but it's worth the wait. Plus, you'll get all that fiber I was talking about!

Ingredients:

  • 1 c soymilk
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 c each blueberry, raspberries
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/2 cup walnuts - chopped
  • 1/4 tsp honey

Nutrition:

  • Protein: 26g
  • Fat: 63g
  • Carbs: 51g

Instructions

  1. At night, mix the milk, chia seeds, and oats in an airtight container
  2. In the morning, mix all the ingredients together
  3. Enjoy!
