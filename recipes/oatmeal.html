Early risers breakfast burrito

If you like eggs, and burritos, then you'll love this burrito. This should take about 15 minutes to make, more if you're like me and can't fold the tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 c mushrooms - sliced
  • 1/4 c yellow onion - chopped
  • 1 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 tbsp salsa
  • 1/4 c cheddar cheese - sliced
  • Salt, pepper for taste

Nutrition:

  • Protein: 32g
  • Fat: 53g
  • Carbs: 38g

Instructions:

  1. Scramble the eggs
  2. Saute the peppers, onions, and mushrooms
  3. Heat the torilla over a burner for about 20 seconds on each side.
  4. Place the ingredients into the tortilla and fold
  5. Enjoy the breakfast!
